Our Menu

Our menu is inspired by our Italian heritage, where everything starts with fresh ingredients. Combine that with the spirit of New York style pizza and subs, and you get a unique blend of food for any occasion. Our different locations have slightly different menus catered to our frequent customers in that area. Each location’s menus are all similar and all have the same attention to freshly prepared pizza and subs.
